The Best Movies According to Joan

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Gangs of Lagos

This movie is so relatable as to what is happening in Nigeria, lagos to be precise, it is so real,and it makes a whole lot of sense to me, as far as the Politics is concerned in my country, everyone has to get their hand dirty to be able to be in the position of power

Brotherhood

In this movie, it talks about how the bond between siblings can be so strong, it is action packed, and relatable as well

Introducing The Kujus

This movie is a comedy that talks about how a typical Nigeria family is, the fights, the bond, and the closure,it is also very relatable and intresting.
